On October 9, 2025, the Ambassador of the Czech Republic to France, Jaroslav Kurfürst, visited the Modane Underground Laboratory (LSM) to express his support for the activities of Czech scientists working there. He also emphasized the Czech side’s interest in continuing and further expanding their collaboration.
